## 2.How many cookies does each user have on average?

```sql
with aeu as 
(select 
	user_id,
	count(cookie_id) as count_cookie
from users	
	group by 1)
	
select 	
	round(avg(count_cookie),2) avg_cookie_users
from aeu
```

| avg_cookie_users |
|------------------|
|             3.56 |
